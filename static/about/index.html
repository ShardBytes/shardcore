In 2018, we won 3rd place in a national game programming competition with our game A Light in The Void.


We are a hopeful future startup consisting of five people who believe in easy to use & quality software for the people.

Starting in 2014, when two of us met in a high school English course. You can say it is quite interesting, when two very shy introverts meet and start looking at each other, can't you :). But curiosity is stronger than insecurity and we began to talk, eventually becoming good friends. Sometime in 2017, we made our first ever commercial application - Library Book Scanner. It wasn't very successful though and never took off. By the beginning of 2018, we welcomed 3 more guys to help us with graphics and music. In April 2018 we launched our first ever game - A Light in The Void - and we won the 3rd place in a nationwide HS & College programming competition. It didn't take too long, until we released our second commercial application in February 2019 - Keksobox - that actually became successful and is now used among a few buffets in Slovakia.
